Our Activities

Waste Segregation Campaigns

Programs that promote segregation of waste at the source of production (household kitchens) and educate people about the benefits of separating biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste

Waste collection & transportation awareness

Programs to raise awareness about the proper collection and transportation of waste, to improve the waste management process.

Publications

Programs that publish a yearly newsletter 'NEPCEMAC SANDESH' and other publications to inform stakeholders, community, as well as minded organizations and service holders regarding environmental issues and waste management.

Public Awareness Programs

Programs that aim to educate and raise awareness about solid waste management issues through various methods such as orientations, trainings, mass campaigns, exhibitions, street dramas, songs, poetry, shows, video documentaries and the use of pamphlets.

Street Sweeping and River Cleaning

Programs that organize cleaning of streets and river banks in areas where door-to-door waste collection services are delivered.

Community Mobilization

Programs that mobilize the community to take active part in improving environmental pollution at the local place.
